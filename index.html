A little about me

Blending technical skills with a designer's eye to build experiences that leave a lasting impression.

✦ ✦ ✦

I'm Nishan Acharya, a Bachelor's student in Computer Engineering at United Technical College under Pokhara University, based in Bharatpur, Chitwan, Nepal. My academic journey has been shaped by a genuine love for both the creative and technical sides of computing.

I focus on building interfaces that communicate clearly, feel intuitive, and look genuinely polished. Whether it's crafting a UI in Figma, styling a layout in CSS, or solving a problem in Python, I bring the same level of care and attention to detail to everything I create.

Outside of code and design, I enjoy exploring the intersection of art and technology, always looking for ideas that can make digital products more human, more beautiful, and more purposeful.
